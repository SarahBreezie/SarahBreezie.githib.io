  • My pencil holder

    • I like this one a lot. Mine is just a painted shoe box with chopped up glad wrap roles that are also painted (blue and black). Because it's shoe box size, all the pencils/pens/scissors/tape etc. that used to be lying on the bench or shoved into a pencil holder that was far to small, now fit in here! I like to think it contributes to a slightly tidier home. :)
